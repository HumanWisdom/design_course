Why do we have different opinions about the same fact?

We have different opinions probably because we have been conditioned differently, by our many influences. We are usually not aware we are conditioned, and yet become attached to those influences, which then shape our opinions, beliefs and our habits.

Understanding our own conditioning helps us understand others better, and make better decisions.
